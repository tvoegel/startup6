As we get older we sometimes need assistance.
Sometimes we will not even able to call for help.

Here this new device will change fundamentally how you can ask for help.

You will not be bothered by technology ...
It is so simple to set up, that everyone can do it.
It is affordable and fits within your budget.
Your data are safe.

How does it work?
Several sensors monitor your activity - if you move,
if you use water, if you get up in the morning. As
you like most people follow a daily routine, a program can establish when you deviate from it in a major way. Then it will alert someone you trust, to find out if everything is ok.

It is a simple as that - and it works. No wires, no hazzle. Just a few sensors.
